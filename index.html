Welcome to Vancouver Flutter Group

Bringing Vancouver Flutter community togehter to learn, connect & create beautiful applications.

About

Flutter is a UI framework by Google to design and build beautiful mobile, web and desktop apps from a single codebase at lightening fast speeds.

Vancouver Flutter Group is formed to bring people together who are using Flutter to create beautiful apps, looking to learn mobile development and making lasting connections.

Our Goals

  • Connect people who are using Flutter or looking to learn Flutter
  • Help with faster onboarding to Flutter with helpful resources and hands on help
  • Inspire and motivate people to work on thier dream project
  • A place to talk about problems and brainstorm ideas
